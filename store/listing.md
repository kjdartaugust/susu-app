## Description (4000)
Susu is the simplest way to run a savings group — the age-old "you save, I collect, then it's your turn" that millions already trust, now organised on your phone.

Set up a circle in seconds: name it, choose how much each member puts in, how often, and the order people collect. Susu tracks every round for you — who has paid, whose turn it is to collect the pot, and how many rounds are left. No more paper lists or arguments about who's up next.

Invite your whole group with a link. When they join, everyone sees the same circle, live — the same members, the same payments, the same schedule. It keeps the group honest and on the same page.

Susu also helps you save on your own. Set goals — school fees, rent, a new phone — and add money toward them whenever you can, watching each one fill up.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Create rotating savings circles (susu / esusu / ajo / tontine)
• Track contributions each round and see who has paid
• See whose turn it is to collect the pot, automatically
• Invite members by link so the whole group shares one circle
• Set personal savings goals and log deposits
• View amounts in Ghana Cedis or US Dollars

HONEST AND PRIVATE
Susu is a record-keeping tool — it helps you organise your group, it does not move, hold, or process money. Your data syncs to your account so you can reach it from any phone, and it's never sold or shared with advertisers.

Made for the way real communities already save.
